Holiday lists

Create new lists

This creates and fills 3 lists. It also adds pre-written holidays to the calendar.

Default

Instructions

  • Create new holidays:
  • Input list name to add it to the accordion.
  • Open it to display its holiday, click a holiday to open its informative link.
  • Click "add holidays" to show all holidays not already added, click a holiday to add it.
  • Click "remove holidays" to show the list's entries, click a holiday to remove it from the list.
  • "Remove list" will delete the entire list (but the holidays will remain on the calendars).
  • The "add pre-written" button will add 3 holidays, and add 50 holidays to the calendars.
